BICOL ADVENTURES



BICOL'S ATTRACTIONS

In Bicol about to ride an ATV!

During this time my family and I were about to ride a very exciting vehicle called an ATV in the rough terrain of Bicol! It was so fun to ride the ATV around the terrrain of Bicol and just absorb the essence and vibes of the nature around me! Truly a must to visit Bicol

BICOL'S BEACHES

A photo taken in a beach I was in at Bicol

This photo I took was one I saw was very aesthetic in a beach in Bicol. It was a very lucky find of the angle of the sun perfectly aligned in the three electrical wires. The beaches of Bicol are beautiful and will truly give you a tan if you need one!

BICOL'S VIEWS

A beautiful view of the Mayon Volcano

This photo was one I took at my aunt and uncle's house on the roof, I saw how beautiful the scenery looked so I took this photo! It shows how beautiful and how much of a must it is to go to Bicol. Not only would you love the views, but the food is absolutely scrumptious as well!
